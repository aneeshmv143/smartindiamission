Objectives

  • To create awareness camp for school students and enhance their knowledge on skill based education which can help in productive employment.
  • To start initiatives in communicating and convincing individuals on the implementation of Smartindia Centers within their area by introducing Vocation / skill based programme and their advantages.
  • Setting up of Smartindia Centers in each and every districts of Kerala / Indian to promote Vocational education programme.
  • To monitor infrastructure facilities in each Smartindia centers as part of skill courses to be conducted.
  • Smartindia Centres will provide career training to enhance their soft skills.
  • Entrepreneurship Development Program will be initiated in all Smartindia Centres across the country.
  • To track the response of students on acquiring skills and get their feedback on the Progress of their skill training.
